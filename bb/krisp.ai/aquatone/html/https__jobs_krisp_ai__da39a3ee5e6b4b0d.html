What is Krisp?

Krisp is a software solution that provides Noise Cancellation, Voice Cancellation, Echo Cancellation, and more to individuals and businesses, helping them to be heard clearer during online meetings in an increasingly remote world.

Mission

Enable people to be more effective communicators in online calls.

Vision

Krisp-powered AI voice technologies are so powerful and effective that they allow billions of people to reduce the gap of their communication skills and become the better version of themselves.

Our values

This system of beliefs guides us in our strategies and everyday decision making.

Be transparent and fair

We believe that full information and decision transparency aligns everyone toward a single mission. We make sure that every decision made at Krisp is fair towards our customers, team members, investors, and the broader communities we serve.

Get things done

We are building a product category that doesn't exist yet, with technologies that others struggle to build. We work hard, learn hard, adapt, and—in the end—always get things done despite any obstacles standing before us.

Move fast

We don’t believe in perfect plans; we believe in action and deliverables. Every day we solve problems that we have never seen in the past. We solve these problems by making small steps and shipping them. We continuously optimize our processes to stay agile and keep up our fast pace.

Exceed expectations

We always go the extra mile to ensure that people have a magical experience when working with us, using our product, and experiencing our brand in any way. We strive to “WOW” everyone around us.

Speak up

Smart people have smart arguments. When we have a reasonable argument, we speak up and challenge the room. At Krisp, the best argument always wins—no matter who is in the room.

Be candid

We are a team with an aligned mission. Every day we help each other to be successful in our roles. When we notice issues, we talk about them candidly, fix them, and move forward.
