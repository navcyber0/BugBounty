Data protection and compliance

The protection of your data is our top priority. Our products are of course DSGVO-compliant and are externally inspected and certified twice a year with regard to information and data security. Any further questions?

We are happy to help you.

Survalyzer offers both simple and highly complex survey solutions with three products: Essential, Professional and Professional Analytics. Based on our 20 years of experience, we also offer comprehensive integration consulting and thus optimally integrate your survey into your corporate landscape.

Survalyzer offers product solutions for simple surveys and highly complex market research requirements. The complexity of the application depends on the problem you want to solve with Survalyzer. Survalyzer places great emphasis on ergonomics and builds user interfaces that are intuitive and user-friendly.

Our free first-level support covers all application questions. In addition, Survalyzer also offers individual onboarding and topic training. For larger licenses, you will also receive a personal contact person who will explain the software step by step and show you solutions to your problems.

Survalyzer meets the highest data protection and compliance requirements. This has been confirmed several times by security audits. TÜV Süd also came to this conclusion in 2020 and certified Survalyzer ISO-27001. Many factors contribute to this level of protection, such as single sign-on (SSO), multifactor authentication (MFA), database encryption, and the annual security test with simulated hacker attacks.
