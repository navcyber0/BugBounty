Open Banking

Open Banking is part of the Australian government’s Consumer Data Rights (CDR) and allow customers to share their data through APIs, promoting and encouraging competition between Australian banks and creating greater transparency for customers. Find more information here on how to get started with Open Banking and access documentation on available APIs.

Bank Feeds with Xero

NAB is the first Australian bank with core API technology linked to Xero’s accounting cloud ecosystems, creating a secure communication between NAB Internet Banking and Xero’s accounting dashboard and allowing small businesses to instantly connect their NAB banking products at the click of a button.

Keep track of your receipts

No more lost receipts or bundles of receipts waiting to be organised. With the NAB app, you simply scan your receipt and it’ll be saved along with all the important purchase details. It’s just the beginning of digital receipts and part of our commitment to helping you manage your finances.
