About Menulog

Menulog is an app-based food delivery service with the greatest choice for Kiwis when it comes to online food delivery. Menulog connects hundreds of thousands active customers with more than 2,000 local restaurants via its websites and native mobile apps. Download the Menulog App via the App Store or Google Play.
