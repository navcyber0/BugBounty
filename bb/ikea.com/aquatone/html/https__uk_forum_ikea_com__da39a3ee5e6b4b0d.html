Closure of the IKEA Community

Hej!

Unfortunately, the IKEA Community is closed as of July 21, 2023. On behalf of all of the IKEA UK Community Managers and Moderators, we’d like to thank you for your contributions, engagement, questions and suggestions over the life of the IKEA Community. TACK! (that’s “thank you” in Swedish!)

Need help? Our customer support team is on hand to help on our Instagram, Twitter and Facebook channels and you can always access our Customer Service hub, where you’ll find answers to hundreds of FAQs, self-serve help options and contact details if you need to reach us via telephone, online chat or if you wish to visit us in store.

IKEA Home smart users can also always find support and reach out to us through the other options in the “Help & Support centre” in the IKEA Home smart apps.

Looking for ideas and inspiration? Visit us on IKEA.UK for endless home furnishing ideas and inspiration to help you Bring Home to Life.
