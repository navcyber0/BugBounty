Does Zerodha support Stockreports+?

As on August 2022, Zerodha has stopped supporting Stockreports+, and new subscriptions will not be accepted.

Zerodha has partnered with Tijori, which provides in-depth financial insights, granular breakup of revenues based on geographies, products, the ability to track prices of raw materials, the timeline for key events for companies, portfolio analysis tools and much more. To learn more, visit tijorifinance.com/timeline/all.
